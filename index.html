Alterations Pinning Certification Program

Train CSRs to pin safely, document accurately, and communicate clearly.

Employee Resource Hub

Complete all five modules, pass the micro-quizzes, and log your results to earn the Alterations Pinning certification.

Program Scope

Covers customer instruction, pinning tools, garment-specific pinning, SPOT POS notes, and escalation rules. Designed for non-tailor CSRs.

  • Certification focus: objective measurements, safety pins, horizontal orientation, clear SPOT notes.
  • Evaluation: complete modules + quizzes, pin 3 garments, accurate SPOT entry, supervisor sign-off.
  • Recertify every 18–24 months or after documentation issues.
How to Use
  1. Enter your name and location/ID.
  2. Complete each module card: overview → objectives → content → quiz → downloads.
  3. Submit quiz to log completion. Results are saved to Google Sheets automatically.
  4. Use the print/PDF button for a completion summary.
